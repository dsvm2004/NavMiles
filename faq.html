Frequently Asked Questions
  • How do I track my miles automatically?
    Tap "Start Tracking" in the app dashboard before your drive. NavMiles will automatically log your trip, miles, and stops. When you arrive, tap "End Tracking" to save your trip to the log. You can also manually enter trips if you forgot to track them.
  • How do I find gas stations nearby?
    Just tap the "Find Gas" button on the main screen. NavMiles instantly shows nearby stations—no searching, no distractions.
  • How do I export my trip logs for taxes?
    Upgrade to the Business plan to unlock trip export. Tap "Export" on your Trip Log screen, choose CSV or PDF, and select your date range. Files are ready for email, print, or upload to your accountant.
  • What features do I get with Business?
    Business unlocks unlimited trip history, IRS-ready PDF & CSV exports, business/personal trip switching, and more. See all upgrade benefits in the app under "Upgrade."
  • Can I switch between business and personal trips?
    Yes! You can tag each trip as "Personal" or "Business" when logging it. You can also view and filter your logs by trip type anytime.
  • Is my data private?
    Absolutely. NavMiles never sells your data or shares trip info with third parties. Your privacy is our priority.
  • How do I contact support?
    Use the "Contact" page in the app or website, or email support@navmiles.com for fast help.
