Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my mileage with NavMiles?

Just open the app, tap "Start Tracking," and drive! NavMiles auto-logs your route, mileage, and duration for you.

How do I export my trip log?

Go to the Trip Log tab and tap "Export"—choose PDF or CSV. For unlimited exports, upgrade to Business!

How does "Find Gas" work?

Tap "Find Gas" to instantly show nearby gas stations along your route. No searching needed!

Is my data private?

Yes, your trip data is stored securely and never sold to third parties. Read our Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel?

Cancel anytime in your device's App Store or Play Store subscriptions section.
