Refund Policy
Last updated: June 2025
Subscription Refunds
All subscription fees and charges for NavMiles are non-refundable except as required by law. If you believe you were charged in error, please contact us at support@navmiles.com within 7 days of the transaction. The maximum refund available for any claim will not exceed the amount paid for your most recent subscription period (monthly payment).
How to Request a Refund
Email support@navmiles.com with your account details and a description of your issue. We'll review your request and respond within 3 business days.
Apple App Store & Google Play
If you purchased NavMiles via the App Store or Google Play, refunds must be requested through their respective platforms, subject to their policies.
No Liability for Indirect Losses
We are not liable for any indirect, incidental, or consequential losses related to refunds. Your sole remedy is a refund of the most recent monthly payment amount, if eligible.
