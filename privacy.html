Privacy Policy
Last Updated: June 2025
1. Introduction
NavMiles (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you use the NavMiles mobile application (“App”).
2. Information We Collect
A. Information You Provide:
• Account Information (name, email, password, vehicle info)
• Payment Information (if/when you purchase subscriptions; processed securely by third-party providers)
• Communications (feedback, support requests)

B. Information Collected Automatically:
• Usage Data (app features used, session length)
• Device Information (type, operating system, unique identifiers)
• Location Data (with your explicit permission—for features such as mileage tracking and fuel finder)
3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
• Provide and improve the NavMiles App and its features
• Process subscriptions and payments (handled by trusted payment partners)
• Communicate with you (including updates, security alerts, customer support)
• Customize your experience and provide relevant suggestions (e.g., fuel stations, route optimization)
• Ensure security and prevent unauthorized use
• Comply with applicable laws and regulations
4. How We Share Your Information
We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information:
• With trusted service providers who help us operate the app (e.g., analytics, payment processors, cloud hosting)
• If required by law, court order, or government request
• To protect our rights, users, property, or safety
• With your consent or at your direction
